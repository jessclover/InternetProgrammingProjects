Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Rock The Town Music Festival!

General Information

Q: What are the festival dates?

A: The festival will be held from June 14-16, 2024.

Q: Where is the festival located?

A: The festival takes place in downtown Columbus, GA, near Riverwalk Park.

Tickets

Q: Are tickets refundable?

A: Tickets are non-refundable, except in the event of festival cancellation.

Q: Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?

A: Yes, tickets can be transferred by contacting our support team.

Parking and Transportation

Q: Is parking available?

A: Parking is available at designated lots downtown. Public transport and rideshares are recommended.

Q: Are shuttles provided?

A: Yes, free shuttle services will run between key locations and the festival site.
