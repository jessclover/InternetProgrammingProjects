Rock The Town Music Festival

June 20-22, 2024 | Downtown Columbus, GA

Event Details

Join us for the ultimate celebration of music, food, and fun at the Rock the Town Music Festival! Experience three days of incredible live performances, delicious food from local vendors, and a vibrant atmosphere in the heart of Downtown Columbus, GA.

Dates: June 20-22, 2024

Location: Downtown Columbus, GA (near Riverwalk Park)

Festival Lineup

Our 2024 lineup features an amazing mix of rock legends and up-and-coming artists. Stay tuned for the full schedule!

  • Day 1: Headliner: The Rolling Thunder | Supporting Acts: The High Notes, Electric Revival
  • Day 2: Headliner: Midnight Echo | Supporting Acts: Sonic Wave, The Rising Stars
  • Day 3: Headliner: Firelight Symphony | Supporting Acts: Golden Strings, Echo Chamber

Food and Drinks

Savor a variety of food and drink options from our local vendors and food trucks, offering everything from classic festival snacks to gourmet meals. Beer, wine, and non-alcoholic beverages will be available for purchase.

Festival Amenities

  • Shaded seating areas
  • On-site restrooms and hygiene stations
  • First aid tents
  • Merchandise booths featuring festival and artist gear
  • Charging stations for electronic devices

Tickets

Tickets are available now! Choose from single-day passes, weekend passes, or VIP packages for the ultimate Rock the Town experience. Visit our Tickets page for details and pricing.

Parking and Transportation

  • Parking is available in designated lots downtown.
  • Free shuttle services will run between key locations and the festival site.
  • We encourage using public transport or rideshares to reduce congestion.
