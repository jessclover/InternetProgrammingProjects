Rock The Town

Downtown Columbus, GA | June 14-16, 2024

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the festival dates?

The festival will take place from June 14th to June 16th, 2024.

Where is the festival located?

Rock The Town is held in downtown Columbus, GA, near Riverwalk Park.

Are tickets refundable?

Tickets are non-refundable, except in the case of festival cancellation.

What items are prohibited at the festival?

Weapons, illegal substances, outside food and beverages, drones, and professional recording equipment are not allowed.

Can I bring my own food and drinks?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed, but the festival features a wide variety of food vendors and beverage options.

Is parking available?

Parking is available at designated lots downtown. We recommend using public transportation or rideshare services to avoid congestion.
